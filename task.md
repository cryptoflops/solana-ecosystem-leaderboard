# Research & Organize Influential Solana Twitter Accounts
Goal: 1st or 2nd Place

**About Superteam Ukraine**
Superteam Ukraine is part of the Superteam, a global community of Solana builders, artists, and creators. We help our members find jobs, get grants, and connect with other talented people in the Solana ecosystem.

**Mission**
Identify and organize influential Solana X (Twitter) accounts that would be valuable for new founders navigating the ecosystem.

**Scope Detail**
Research and collect Twitter accounts of individuals and projects important to the Solana ecosystem. This includes:

Founders of Solana projects

OGs (Original Gangsters/long-time contributors)

Builders and developers

Researchers

Business Development (BD) roles

Marketing specialists

Anyone else deemed important for new founders to know

Categorize or group these accounts logically.

Provide a simple CSV file that contains the Group Name, link to the Twitter account, the account holder's Name, and a Note.

Submission Requirements
A spreadsheet (xlsx/csv) file containing all the collected and categorized X (Twitter) accounts.

Minimum of 50 unique Twitter accounts.

All entries must be in English.

**Judging Criteria**
Relevance: How well do the selected accounts align with the goal of being important for new Solana founders?

Comprehensiveness: The breadth and depth of the accounts collected across different roles and segments of the Solana ecosystem.

Accuracy: Correct Twitter links and accurate information in the 'Name' and 'Note' fields.

Clarity of Categorization: How logical and useful are the chosen group names?

Conciseness of Notes: Are the notes brief yet informative, providing clear value for why each account is important?

Formatting: Adherence to the requested CSV format.

**Reward Structure**
1st Place: 200 USDG

2nd Place: 150 USDG

Bonus Awards: 3 winners receive 50 USDG each
